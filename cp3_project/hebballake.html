About This Location

Hebbal Lake: Built in the 16th century, Hebbal Lake was once an irrigation reservoir for the area. Over time, it became an important spot for migratory birds and nature lovers. The lake is surrounded by lush greenery and offers scenic walking paths, making it a peaceful retreat for Bangaloreans. The spot is also a haven for birdwatching, with over 70 species recorded.
