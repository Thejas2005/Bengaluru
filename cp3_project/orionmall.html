About This Location

Orion Mall: Orion Mall, opened in 2012, is a luxury shopping and entertainment complex situated near the famous Mantri Mall. It features a vast array of international and local brands, along with a multiplex cinema and several dining options. The mall is designed with modern architecture, including an open-air promenade overlooking a lake. It is a popular hangout spot for families, shoppers, and moviegoers.
