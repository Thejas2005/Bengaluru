About This Location

Gavi Gangadhareshwara Temple: Gavi Gangadhareshwara Temple is an ancient cave temple dating back to the 9th century, built by the Chola dynasty. The temple is dedicated to Lord Shiva and is famous for its unique architecture, with rock-cut caves and a natural sunlight phenomenon that illuminates the idol during specific times of the year. Located at the foot of a hill, it also features intricate carvings, making it an architectural marvel in Bangalore.
